About

Research at the intersection of Earth observation, freshwater science, and environmental decision-making.

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow and Contract Teaching Faculty member in the Department of Geography and Environmental Studies at Wilfrid Laurier University. I also serve as Administrator of the Cold Regions Research Centre.

My research develops long-term environmental datasets and scalable geospatial workflows for northern lakes. I work across lake surface temperature, ice thickness and phenology, turbidity, water quality, climate trends, and landscape change.

My background spans academia, environmental consulting, conservation GIS, agricultural field mapping, software development, and university teaching in Canada, Germany, and Ghana.

Selected research

Research programs and technical contributions

Great Slave Lake

Remote sensing · Machine learning · Water quality

Long-term turbidity monitoring for Great Slave Lake

Developed a dual-threshold random forest framework that harmonizes MODIS and VIIRS observations to retrieve turbidity, examine seasonal patterns, identify spatial regimes, and quantify long-term change across Great Slave Lake.

PythonGoogle Earth EngineRandom ForestMODISVIIRS

North Slave Region

Lake ice · Climate change · Spatial modelling

Lake ice thickness and phenology across 535 northern lakes

Produced spatially distributed estimates of ice thickness, freeze-up, break-up, and ice-cover duration using Landsat-derived lake surface temperature, ERA5 climate data, and thermodynamic modelling.

Northern Lake Temperature

Earth observation · Climate records · Open data

Four decades of lake surface temperature

Created and analysed a lake-specific Landsat temperature archive for the North Slave Region, generating a reproducible record of thermal conditions from 1984 to 2021.

GeoAI and Land Change

Classification · Remote sensing · Machine learning

Machine learning for surface mining and landscape change

Evaluated supervised machine-learning models using Sentinel-2 imagery to detect and map surface mining, extending earlier work in environmental monitoring, vegetation analysis, and automated GIS processing.
